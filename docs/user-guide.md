## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the PixelFlow AI website at [https://pixelflow.ai](https://pixelflow.ai)
2. Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Click "Sign Up" to create your account
5. Check your email for a verification link and click it to verify your account

### Logging In

1. Visit the PixelFlow AI website at [https://pixelflow.ai](https://pixelflow.ai)
2. Click the "Log In" button in the top right corner
3. Enter your email address and password
4. Click "Log In" to access your account

### Uploading an Image

1. After logging in, you'll be taken to the main editor page
2. If you don't have an image open, you'll see the upload area
3. Click the "Upload Image" button or drag and drop an image onto the upload area
4. Your image will be uploaded and displayed in the editor

## Basic Image Editing

### Adjusting Brightness, Contrast, and Saturation

1. With an image open in the editor, locate the "Adjustments" panel on the left side
2. Use the sliders to adjust the following properties:
   - **Brightness**: Increase or decrease the overall lightness of the image
   - **Contrast**: Increase or decrease the difference between light and dark areas
   - **Saturation**: Increase or decrease the intensity of colors
   - **Blur**: Add a blur effect to the image
   - **Sepia**: Add a sepia tone effect to the image
   - **Hue Rotate**: Shift all colors in the image

3. The changes will be applied to the image in real-time
4. To reset all adjustments, click the "Reset" button at the bottom of the panel

### Using the Zoom and Pan Tools

1. With an image open in the editor, use the zoom controls in the toolbar above the image:
   - Click the "Zoom In" button to zoom in
   - Click the "Zoom Out" button to zoom out
   - Click the "Fit" button to fit the image to the editor
   - Click the "100%" button to view the image at actual size

2. To pan around the image when zoomed in:
   - Click and drag the image to move it around
   - Use the scroll wheel to zoom in and out

## AI-Powered Features

### Removing Backgrounds

1. With an image open in the editor, locate the "AI Tools" panel on the left side
2. Click the "Remove Background" button
3. The AI will process the image and remove the background
4. The background will be replaced with transparency
5. You can then export the image with a transparent background or add a new background

### Auto-Enhancing Images

1. With an image open in the editor, locate the "AI Tools" panel on the left side
2. Click the "Auto Enhance" button
3. The AI will analyze the image and apply optimal adjustments
4. The enhancements will be applied to the image automatically
5. You can further refine the adjustments using the sliders in the "Adjustments" panel

### Applying Artistic Filters (Pro/Premium Feature)

1. With an image open in the editor, locate the "AI Tools" panel on the left side
2. Click the "Artistic Style" button (requires Pro or Premium subscription)
3. Select a style from the list of available styles
4. The AI will process the image and apply the selected style
5. You can adjust the intensity of the effect using the slider

### AI Upscaling (Premium Feature)

1. With an image open in the editor, locate the "AI Tools" panel on the left side
2. Click the "AI Upscale" button (requires Premium subscription)
3. Select the desired upscale factor (2x, 4x, etc.)
4. The AI will process the image and increase its resolution
5. The upscaled image will replace the original in the editor

## Exporting Your Work

### Exporting as PNG or JPG

1. With an edited image open in the editor, click the "Export" button in the toolbar
2. In the Export Options modal, select the desired format:
   - **PNG**: Best for images with transparency or when quality is important
   - **JPG**: Best for smaller file sizes when transparency is not needed

3. If you selected JPG, use the Quality slider to adjust the compression level
4. Select the desired size:
   - **Original Size**: Keep the original dimensions
   - **Large (2048px)**: Good for printing
   - **Medium (1024px)**: Web optimized
   - **Small (512px)**: Social media

5. Click the "Export Image" button to download the file

### Sharing Your Images

1. With an edited image open in the editor, click the "Share" button in the toolbar
2. In the Sharing Options modal, select how you want to share:
   - **Copy Link**: Copy a link to the image to share with others
   - **Download**: Download the image to your device
   - **Twitter**: Share the image on Twitter
   - **Facebook**: Share the image on Facebook
   - **LinkedIn**: Share the image on LinkedIn

3. Follow the prompts to complete the sharing process

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Image Upload Fails

- Ensure the image is in a supported format (JPG, PNG, WebP)
- Check that the image file size is under 10MB
- Try a different browser or device
- Clear your browser cache and try again

#### AI Tools Not Working

- Check your internet connection
- Ensure you have enough edits remaining (Free tier) or an active subscription (Pro/Premium)
- Try a different image (some images may be too complex for certain AI tools)
- Wait a few minutes and try again (the AI service may be temporarily busy)

#### Export or Sharing Fails

- Check your internet connection
- Try a different export format or size
- Clear your browser cache and try again
- Ensure you have enough storage space on your device (for downloads)

### Getting Help

If you encounter any issues not covered in this guide:

1. Click the "Help" button in the bottom right corner
2. Search the knowledge base for answers to common questions
3. If you can't find a solution, click "Contact Support" to submit a support ticket
4. Provide as much detail as possible about the issue, including:
   - What you were trying to do
   - What happened instead
   - Any error messages you received
   - Screenshots if possible

Our support team will respond to your ticket as soon as possible.
